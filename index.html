Building a Strong Startup Ecosystem in Kerala

Talrop is building a strong startup ecosystem in Kerala and aims to transform Kerala into a Silicon Valley model by 2030. Talrop has introduced various projects and programs to fulfil this goal.

140 Startups from Kerala

We aim to build 140 startups from Kerala by the end of 2025 to shape and strengthen the startup ecosystem, and we have already launched 10 startups and the remaining 130 will be launched in the upcoming years.

Kerala needs a strong
Startup Ecosystem!

The backbone of Kerala's economy is the incoming foreign funds. To accelerate its influx, our products and services need to reach the international market. This is possible when there are more entrepreneurs and new innovative startups.

The current ecosystem in Kerala is providing good support for startups, to which Talrop intends to add more supportive systems and make it even more strong and efficient for promoting startups and its activities. The different aspects of starting a venture, like its idea validation, team development, funding, etc. are not hassle-free as they should be. This is why Kerala needs a strong startup ecosystem to build more startups and promote our economic growth.
